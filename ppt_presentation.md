# Palo Alto Networks: Employee Attrition AI Engine
**Executive Presentation**

---

## Slide 1: Title
**Predictive HR Analytics**
*Using AI to Score Employee Attrition Risk Before It Happens*
*Presenter: Senior ML Engineer & HR Analytics Consultant*

---

## Slide 2: The Problem
**Reactive vs. Proactive Retention**
- **Loss of Talent**: Unexpected resignations disrupt workflows.
- **Data Silos**: HR metrics live in spreadsheets without predictive synergy.
- **Late Efforts**: Retention bonuses offered after a resignation are less effective and more expensive.

---

## Slide 3: The AI Solution
**Predictive Attrition & Risk Scoring Engine**
- **Data Integration**: Combines Overtime, Salary, Manager Relationships, and Work-Life balance.
- **Smart Features**: Engineered metrics like the *Manager Relationship Score* and *Income-to-Experience Ratio*.
- **Machine Learning**: Uses highly tuned Logistic Regression models connected with Synthethic Minority Over-sampling Technique (SMOTE).

---

## Slide 4: Real-world ROI
**Empowering HR with Explainable AI**
- Every employee gets a score from 0 to 100.
- Categorization into **Low, Medium, High Risk**.
- Reason Codes generated automatically: (e.g., "High Risk due to 3+ years Without Promotion and High Overtime").

---

## Slide 5: Live Dashboard Demonstration
**"What-If" Simulator**
- HR Business Partners can log in and simulate targeted interventions.
- "If we give Employee #12 a 15% salary bump, does their risk drop?"
- See instantaneous shifts in predicted probabilities.

---

## Slide 6: Deployment & Next Steps
- **Q3 Rollout**: AWS/Docker deployment accessible via internal SSO.
- **Continuous Learning**: As more HR data flows securely, the AI pipeline will auto-retrain and improve accuracy.
- **Goal**: Reduce overall unexpected turnover by 15% in year 1.
